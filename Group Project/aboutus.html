ABOUT US

Wrap & Go is a unique online shopping service based in Derry, Northern Ireland. The business was founded in order to make thoughtful gifting in Derry simple.
Wrap & Go is here to take all the hassle out of finding an original present to ensure the recipient is left with a smile on their face. It is our purpose to create beautiful gifts that are full of real thought and imagination, while making sure that you as a busy person get an opportunity to regain free time from hassle of choosing a gift.
We offer a wide variety of gifts for all kinds of recipients, for him, for her or for a child. No matter which category you choose, we will guarantee that your gift will be leaving a smile!
